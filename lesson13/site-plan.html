Overview

Purpose

To inform the public of the temples and know what ordinance, where they are, what time they are open and the name of each temple is. We love it when people go to the temple to perform ordinances, pray, visit and possibly find answers to many of their prayers and questions. This website will help them find out the information necessary to attend and be apart of the House Of The Lord.

Audience

The audience I'm targeting are members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints so that they may be informed when the temple is open, where the temple is and what is being done in the temple. As well as what ordinances are being performed at this temple.
